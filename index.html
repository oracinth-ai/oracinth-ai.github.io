Platform positioning

The AI operating system for logistics execution, visibility, and decision-making.

Logistics breaks when data, teams, and decisions stay disconnected.

Most logistics businesses still run critical operations across multiple systems, emails, spreadsheets, and manual handoffs. Shipment updates live in one tool. Billing data lives in another. Support teams work in inboxes. Planners use spreadsheets. Leadership sees reports too late.

This creates delays, missed exceptions, invoice leakage, poor customer experience, low asset utilization, and slow decision-making.

Oracinth solves this by turning disconnected logistics operations into one live, action-ready system.

Platform suites

The product architecture behind Oracinth MotionOS.

Oracinth MotionOS is organized into seven product suites so logistics teams can connect fragmented data, model real operations, run workflows, deploy AI, collaborate across partners, and govern every action from one live system.

Data foundation

Oracinth PulseGrid

Connect live APIs, files, events, and records from TMS, WMS, ERP, finance, CRM, telematics, email, and partner systems.

Operational ontology

Oracinth RealityGraph

Model logistics as live business objects such as shipments, routes, assets, invoices, exceptions, tasks, customers, and vendors.

Execution workspace

Oracinth ActionDesk

Give operators, planners, finance teams, and managers one workspace to investigate, decide, act, and write updates back to systems.

AI suite

Oracinth Cognition Studio

Deploy LogicWorks, AgentDock, DocStream AI, CommandChat, ModelSwitch, and EvalForge inside real logistics workflows.

Decision layer

Oracinth SignalCore

Use SignalWatch, RootLens, and ScenarioOps to detect risk, investigate patterns, and improve operational decisions.

Ecosystem collaboration

Oracinth PartnerMesh

Collaborate securely across shippers, carriers, warehouses, customs brokers, and service providers in governed shared workflows.

Governance layer

Oracinth TrustLayer

Control access, monitor activity, govern AI, enforce approvals, and maintain full operational visibility across every workflow.
