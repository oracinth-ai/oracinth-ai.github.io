Products

Product suites designed for logistics execution and decision-making.

Oracinth MotionOS is organized into seven product suites. Each suite has a distinct role in data integration, operational modeling, workflow execution, AI, decision support, ecosystem collaboration, and governance.

Data integration suite

Oracinth PulseGrid

Connect the systems your operations already depend on.

PulseGrid is Oracinth's integration backbone. It unifies transport, warehouse, finance, CRM, telematics, document, and partner data into one continuously updated operational foundation.

What it offers: Unified data ingestion, transformation, standardization, quality controls, and live operational synchronization.

How it is offered: Through connectors, pipelines, event ingestion, data rules, and governed data modeling.

Connectors HubPipeline DesignerLive Event StreamData Quality RulesMaster Data HarmonizationUnified Logistics Data Layer

Use case: combine TMS shipment events, GPS pings, depot scans, finance records, and customer complaints into one real-time operational stream.

Operational ontology suite

Oracinth RealityGraph

Model your logistics business the way it actually runs.

RealityGraph transforms disconnected data into business objects and relationships that operators, managers, AI functions, and workflows can all understand.

What it offers: Business object modeling, object relationships, state transitions, object history, search, operational context, and action permissions.

How it is offered: As a live object and relationship layer on top of integrated operational data.

Shipment Object ModelRoute & Lane GraphAsset & Fleet GraphCustomer & Vendor GraphInvoice & Claims GraphState & Status Engine

Use case: open a shipment and see linked order, carrier, temperature events, SLA, documents, and recovery tasks in one place.

Workflow and apps suite

Oracinth ActionDesk

Give teams one place to detect, decide, and act.

ActionDesk provides operational applications for the people doing the work. It supports queues, case handling, escalations, approvals, and direct system updates.

What it offers: Control tower screens, workflow steps, task queues, approvals, assignments, escalations, and writeback actions.

How it is offered: As role-based operational workspaces built on top of RealityGraph.

Control Tower ViewsException QueuesCase ManagementApproval WorkflowsDispatch BoardsSystem Writeback Connectors

Use case: rank shipment exception cases by customer priority, delay severity, and financial impact in one operational workspace.

Enterprise AI suite

Oracinth Cognition Studio

Deploy AI that understands logistics context and supports real operations.

Cognition Studio is Oracinth's AI suite. It includes AI functions, agents, document intelligence, conversational operations, model routing, and evaluation controls designed for enterprise execution.

What it offers: Operational AI functions, agents, document parsing, conversational investigation, model control, and safe release testing.

How it is offered: As a governed AI layer connected to live business objects, workflows, permissions, and documents.

Oracinth LogicWorksOracinth AgentDockOracinth DocStream AIOracinth CommandChatOracinth ModelSwitchOracinth EvalForge

Use case: a finance AI reviews a disputed invoice, links it to shipment milestones and contract terms, drafts an explanation, and routes the case to the right approver.

Alerting and optimization suite

Oracinth SignalCore

Detect risk, investigate patterns, and optimize decisions.

SignalCore helps logistics teams move from reactive firefighting to active control. It surfaces risk, explains recurring problems, and recommends better allocation choices.

What it offers: Alerts, anomaly detection, cohort analysis, root cause investigation, scenario planning, and optimization workflows.

How it is offered: Through event monitoring, business rules, AI signals, and scenario engines connected to operational workflows.

Oracinth SignalWatchOracinth RootLensOracinth ScenarioOps

Use case: identify rising dwell times at one warehouse and recommend schedule adjustments for recovery.

Multi-organization collaboration suite

Oracinth PartnerMesh

Coordinate across the logistics ecosystem without losing control.

PartnerMesh enables shared workflows across carriers, warehouse operators, vendors, customs brokers, and enterprise customers while preserving controlled visibility.

What it offers: Partner portals, shared cases, external approvals, document exchange, and cross-organization workflow collaboration.

How it is offered: As secure multi-party workspaces connected to internal operational objects and rules.

Carrier PortalShipper Collaboration WorkspaceVendor Task ExchangeExternal Document ExchangeShared Workflow ApprovalsEcosystem Visibility Layer

Use case: a delayed international shipment case is shared across the shipper, carrier, and customs broker with tracked next steps.

Governance and observability suite

Oracinth TrustLayer

Run critical logistics workflows with security, control, and accountability.

TrustLayer provides role-based permissions, object-level access control, monitoring, AI observability, audit history, and approval frameworks for sensitive operations.

What it offers: Security, governance, observability, operational monitoring, AI controls, and compliance support.

How it is offered: As a cross-platform governance layer applied to users, objects, workflows, and AI systems.

Role & Policy EngineObject-Level PermissionsAudit TrailsWorkflow MonitoringAI ObservabilityDecision LoggingApproval Controls

Use case: an AI-generated recommendation can be viewed by an analyst, but only a finance lead can approve a settlement above the allowed threshold.
