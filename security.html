Security & Governance

Security, governance, and control built into every workflow.

Oracinth is designed for enterprise logistics operations where visibility, access, approval, and accountability matter.

Govern operations, users, actions, and AI from one control layer

Every shipment, invoice, case, document, recommendation, and action inside Oracinth can be secured, monitored, and audited. Access can be defined by role, team, geography, business function, account ownership, or workflow stage.

Built-in trust for operational systems

Role-based access

Control who can view, edit, approve, or act.

Object-level permissions

Restrict access to specific customers, regions, case types, or financial data.

Audit trails

Track who saw what, changed what, approved what, and when.

AI governance

Monitor AI outputs, review agent behavior, and keep humans in control of sensitive decisions.

Observability

Measure workflow performance, system activity, and operational bottlenecks.

Approval frameworks

Set escalation and approval rules for financial, customer, and operational decisions.

Built for real operational accountability

Oracinth does not separate intelligence from control. The same platform that helps teams act also ensures the right people stay responsible for important decisions.
