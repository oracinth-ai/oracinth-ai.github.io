Solutions

Solutions built around the hardest logistics workflows.

Oracinth helps logistics businesses solve operational problems that are urgent, repetitive, high-value, and cross-functional. Each solution combines the right Oracinth MotionOS product suites for the workflow.

Shipment Exception Management

Operations teams often discover delays too late because information is scattered across tracking systems, customer calls, and carrier updates.

Oracinth detects risk early, ranks issues by business impact, gathers context automatically, and guides teams through recovery actions.

Sample: a key shipment misses a hub scan and Oracinth raises the case with route context and recommended escalation steps.

Powered by Oracinth SignalCore, Oracinth ActionDesk, and Oracinth RealityGraph.

Control Tower Operations

Control tower teams often depend on disconnected screens and manual communication.

Oracinth unifies shipments, assets, delays, partner events, documents, alerts, and tasking into one action-ready environment.

Sample: a supervisor sees all priority exceptions across regions and assigns owners from one screen.

Powered by Oracinth ActionDesk, Oracinth RealityGraph, and Oracinth SignalCore.

Invoice Disputes and Revenue Recovery

Finance teams navigate contracts, shipment records, invoices, and customer communication across many systems.

Oracinth creates one dispute case with linked invoice lines, service events, contract references, extracted documents, AI explanations, and approvals.

Sample: a customer disputes detention charges and Oracinth links timestamps, contract rules, and supporting evidence.

Powered by Oracinth Cognition Studio, Oracinth ActionDesk, Oracinth RealityGraph, and Oracinth TrustLayer.

Load, Route, and Capacity Optimization

Planning decisions are often manual, spreadsheet-driven, and too slow to respond to changing realities.

Oracinth recommends consolidation, sequencing, dispatch planning, and capacity allocation based on constraints, costs, and service impact.

Sample: identify partial loads that can be combined into a more profitable route.

Powered by Oracinth SignalCore, Oracinth RealityGraph, and Oracinth ActionDesk.

Document Intelligence for Logistics

Invoices, PODs, bills, emails, and contracts arrive in many formats and consume valuable team time.

Oracinth extracts, classifies, validates, and attaches document intelligence directly to business workflows.

Sample: POD documents are read, matched to shipments, checked for missing signatures, and routed automatically.

Powered by Oracinth Cognition Studio, Oracinth RealityGraph, and Oracinth ActionDesk.

Investigation and Root Cause Analysis

Recurring delays and leakage often stay hidden because teams only solve case by case.

Oracinth analyzes issues by lane, carrier, customer, shift, depot, service type, or asset behavior to expose patterns.

Sample: repeated service failures traced to one subcontractor on one lane with poor dock coordination.

Powered by Oracinth SignalCore, Oracinth RealityGraph, and Oracinth PulseGrid.

Partner Collaboration

Cross-organization coordination is often slow, fragmented, and hard to track.

Oracinth uses shared workspaces, controlled access, document exchange, and tracked approvals across partners.

Sample: a customs hold case is worked jointly by shipper, broker, and carrier.

Powered by Oracinth PartnerMesh, Oracinth ActionDesk, and Oracinth TrustLayer.

Fleet Maintenance and Warranty Recovery

Maintenance, parts usage, and warranty claims are often handled in separate systems with weak visibility.

Oracinth connects maintenance events, asset history, service contracts, supplier terms, and claim opportunities in one workflow.

Sample: recurring component failure is matched to an active supplier warranty clause.

Powered by Oracinth PulseGrid, Oracinth RealityGraph, Oracinth ActionDesk, and Oracinth SignalCore.

AI Copilots and Agents for Logistics Teams

Generic copilots answer questions but cannot handle operational context safely.

Oracinth deploys role-based AI functions and agents grounded in shipments, invoices, tasks, documents, and rules.

Sample: a service agent asks for the highest-risk customer cases today and receives ranked next-step suggestions.

Powered by Oracinth Cognition Studio, Oracinth RealityGraph, Oracinth ActionDesk, and Oracinth TrustLayer.
